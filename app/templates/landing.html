// What WebVulnScan Detects
Port & Service Scan

Identifies open ports and exposed network services on the target host that could serve as entry points for attackers.

SQL Injection · XSS · LFI

Tests web inputs and parameters for injection flaws — including SQLi, Cross-Site Scripting, and Local File Inclusion vulnerabilities.

Exposed Files & Paths

Crawls the target to surface sensitive files, admin panels, backup directories, and misconfigured endpoints left publicly accessible.

Subdomain Enumeration

Discovers active subdomains to map the full attack surface — revealing forgotten or unprotected parts of the target infrastructure.
